Key Concepts

Velocity vs Acceleration

Velocity is the rate of change of position. Acceleration is the rate of change of velocity.

Newton's Laws of Motion

Newton's First Law states that an object at rest will stay at rest unless acted upon by an external force. The Second Law (F = ma) shows how force and acceleration are related. The Third Law states that every action has an equal and opposite reaction.

Energy & Work

Energy is the ability to do work. Work is done when a force is applied to an object and it moves in the direction of the force.
